About Brett

I'm a Talented Guy who's entering his career as a Software Engineer! I enjoy solving problems and running the numbers to produce the highest quality product. I have a background in brewery operations, logistics and eye for detail. While in the brewery industry i took the time to better understand spreadsheet functionality, and used that to hand craft many tools for areas such as inventory & distribution management.

As it turns out, I really enjoy designing spreadsheets and working through a problem. That realization gave me the confidence boost needed to change careers and start clearing my path towards life as a software engineer.

BrewMore

Innovating Craft Beer Tools For Everyone

Ever wonder how much material you will need to complete your project? BrewMore does that by offering breweries a profitable outlook on ordering materials and storage of brand recipes by decreasing the time required to order ingredients, reducing operation overheard, and freeing up assets for future development.

  • Brewers can:
  • Create a digitial recipe card for each brand of beer
  • Add potential brews of each recipe to an ordering page,
  • Then Print a report containing all grain required for those brews, with weights listed in pounds and bags
